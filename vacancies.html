We Are Hiring

We are looking for passionate, skilled teachers to join our team. We are also in need of enthusiastic parents and others who are willing to promote our programs to others across the world. Parents everywhere are making big sacrifices to give the best education to their children, but school education as we know is not enough anymore - that's why we are in need enthusiastic educators, parents and others to promote this research and programs in all parts of the world.


There are several ways that you can earn an income through Ruwan Education and help spread the word, namely:

  • Promoting our packages & workshops

  • Promoting our books as an affiliate

  • Hosting our workshops in your town

  • Serving as a Licensee

  • Translating of our books, courses and material

  • Virtual Assistance/Content Writing/ Digital Marketing

  • Webinar Organisers

  • Event/Business Development Managing

  • Educators of Education/ Pre-School facilities

  • Owners of Pre-School Centres

If you are interested in joining our family and making a difference in the lives of children & students across the world, or want an independent income, contact us or fill in the form below and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
